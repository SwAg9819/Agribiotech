Our Story

Rooted in Science,
Grown for Farmers

AgriBiotech-India was born from a simple belief: that Indian farmers deserve better than chemical inputs that damage their soil and health.

Based in Saradapally, Hooghly, West Bengal, we specialize in bio-organic fertilizers and natural pesticides — designed to replace harmful chemicals while dramatically improving crop yields and preserving biodiversity.

Our mission is to put cutting-edge agricultural biotechnology in the hands of rural farmers — affordably, accessibly, and sustainably.
